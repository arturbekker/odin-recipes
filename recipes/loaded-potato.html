Dirty Loaded Potato

Description

A loaded potato that is a bit too loaded and looks quite messy, but I promise it tastes better than it looks.

This recipe will be the current variation I make myself, but it can be customized any way you like.

Ingredients

  • Large Baking Potato
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Shredded Rotiserrie Chicken
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Chop up half an onion (or more or less, to your taste) and mince two or three cloves of garlic.
  2. Carmelize the onions on a pan, using a small amount of olive oil. Add the garlic into the pan once the onions are just about done carmelizing. This will take around 15 minutes.
  3. Clean your potato and poke holes using a fork. Put the potato on a microwave-safe plate and put it in the microwave for 5 minutes. After 5 minutes, flip the potato and put it back in the microwave for another 3 minutes.
  4. Cut open your potato, and mash it down on your plate. You can either try to keep the potato intact or just mash it all down to create a flat surface.
  5. Get about half a tablespoon of unsalted butter and spread it on the potato.
  6. Generously sprinkle salt and pepper on the potato.
  7. Add the carmelized onions and garlic to the potato.
  8. Add a heap of shredded cheese to the potato.
  9. Add your warm shredded rotiserrie chicken to the potato.
  10. Mix all your toppings up.
  11. Enjoy!
